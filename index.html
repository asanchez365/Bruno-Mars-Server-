Bruno Mars

Jazmin Martinez & Aaron Sanchez

Bruno Mars was born on October 8, 1985. He was born in Honolulu, Hawaii and is 38 years old. Bruno Mars has 5 siblings! His real name is Peter Gene Hernandez; Bruno Mars is his stage name! Growing up he wanted to be a solo music artist and did everything he could to make that happen! Bruno Mars had released three albums; Doo-Wops & Hooligans, Unorthodox Jukebox, and 24K Magic. Bruno Mars is known for his very bipolar songs; one song can be super sad and another song can be very energetic. His songs are very meaningful and most people can relate to him. You are missing out in life if you do not listen to Bruno Mars!
